2. The Service

Nexus is a multi-tenant, subscription-based platform comprising the following core modules:

  • Core — employee master data, departments, positions, document storage, and user authentication
  • Attendance & Leave — mobile clock-in/clock-out, shift management, and leave requests and approvals
  • HSE & Man-Hours — safety violation tracking, man-hours aggregation, induction management, work permits, and license tracking
  • Payroll — payroll calculation, payslip generation, overtime, and statutory deductions (including PPh 21 and BPJS, as applicable in Indonesia)

The Service is accessed via (a) a mobile application ("Mobile App"), used by all personnel — including employees, managers, and administrators — for clock-in/clock-out and self-service functions, and (b) a web dashboard ("Web Dashboard"), used by managers and HR administrators for employee records, payroll processing, HSE oversight, and approval workflows.
