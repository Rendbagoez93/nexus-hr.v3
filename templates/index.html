Four modules. One employee record.

Every module references a single Employee entity — no duplicate data, no sync errors, no more guessing which system is right.

🧑‍💼

Core

Employee master data, departments, positions, documents, and authentication.

  • Single source of truth per employee
  • Document storage with signed URLs
  • Role-based access control
🕒

Attendance & Leave

Mobile clock-in/out with GPS and photo verification, shift management, and leave requests and approvals.

  • Fraud-resistant clock-in with GPS geofencing and photo verification
  • Shift-aware live status
  • Leave requests and balances
🦺

HSE + Man Hours

Safety violations with photo evidence, man-hour aggregation, inductions, and multi-step work permit tracking.

  • Induction and licence expiry alerts
  • Man-hour reporting
  • Work permit lifecycle
💰

Payroll

Payroll calculation, payslip generation, overtime, PPh 21, and BPJS deductions — calculated correctly, every cycle.

  • Indonesian tax & BPJS compliance
  • Overtime and payslip history
  • Auditable payroll runs
